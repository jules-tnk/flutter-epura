Trust Center

Cleanup stays local and deliberate.

Epura is built around a simple rule: the app should help you manage files on your phone without turning your phone into a data source.

Local by design

Epura does not require an account, does not collect analytics, and does not upload file lists, file names, paths, folder names, diagnostic reports, or review history.

User-controlled access

Media access is used to show photos and videos for review. Custom folders and imported downloads are available only after you pick them with Android's system picker.

  • You choose what Epura reviews.
  • You can remove custom folders from Settings.
  • Clearing Epura history does not delete files or revoke folder access.

Deletion is explicit

Epura never auto-deletes. Swipe decisions are reviewed at the end of a session, and deletion happens only after confirmation.

Local history controls

The privacy and permissions page shows what Epura can access and lets you clear Epura history without deleting files or changing folder grants.

What Epura does not do

  • No account system.
  • No telemetry, analytics, or hidden uploads.
  • No app inventory scanning or usage access.
  • No cloud sync, backup, or remote delete.
